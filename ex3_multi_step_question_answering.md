# Objective: Break complex questions into smaller steps.

> Select a multi-step question (e.g., "What is 25% of the sum of 40 and 60?").

## A student scored 45 marks in Mathematics and 55 marks in English.
> What is the average score, and what is 10% of that average?

> Create a prompt that asks the model to:

> Restate the problem

### Step 1: Restate the problem

> Find the average of the two scores (45 and 55).

> Find 10% of that average.

## Break it into smaller steps

### Step 2: Calculate total score

Add the marks together:
45 + 55 = 100

### Step 3: Calculate average

Average = Total ÷ Number of subjects
100 ÷ 2 = 50

## Solve each step before giving the final answer

### Step 4: Calculate 10% of the average

10% of 50 = (10 ÷ 100) × 50 = 5

### Final Answer

Average score: 50

10% of the average: 5
